The Life Change Strategy

Blaze

Finding accommodation and Family Insertion: LCM is focusing on homeless people by facilitating a place to stay (shelters, housing, room for rent and family insertion). Methodology: Field work (personal advices on the streets), Haven Night Shelter, Municipality facility for Housing Application, Gumtree, Shoprite and Picknpay for ads on rooms, and phone calls and assistance on transport for family contacts or visits.

Highlight

Substance abuse and HIV/Aids counseling: LCM provides counseling and workshops sessions around issues such as drugs, alcohol abuse and HIV/aids to help those victims to understand their negative effects and remedies. Methodology: Chemistry and physiological knowledge of Substance Abuse (qualified Medicinal Chemist does that) and another staff with Honours in Psychology is assisting us on counseling. LCM also encourages them to attend church services around the area and our Mondays Supportive group (workshops with current and former drug users, alcoholic, struggling financially and other issues) by allowing people to share their problems, encourage one another by the word of God or any word and praying for one another, and foods assistance (soup kitchen) are generally shared afterwards.

Empowerment

Computer literacy, Business development, money skills and Jobs hunting: LCM assists homeless to find work by CV writing, State document application or renewal, providing transport on job hunting and first month of work. We also empower them with money skills enables them to manage wisely their finances. LCM identifies talented people and encourages them to develop that. The skills obtained help them to get secure job or start a small business. LCM teaches disadvantaged to fish rather than begging daily. As result after a period of time some became very independents from streets, shelters and society
